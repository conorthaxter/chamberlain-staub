About

Visually driven,
character-focused
storytelling.

Chamberlain Staub has produced Emmy winning series, including Chef's Table and Live to 100: Secrets of the Blue Zones, developing a foundation in visually driven, character-focused storytelling.

She leans into a hybrid directorial style blending documentary and scripted elements. Her latest directorial project, Doom, created for the band Great Grandpa, explores emotional inheritance and the feeling of being trapped inside unresolved pasts.
